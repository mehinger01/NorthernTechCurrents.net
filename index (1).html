About Mike Ehinger

Built on real experience,
not just credentials

I am a northern Michigan educator who has spent 12+ years leading school districts and the past year and a half training AI for leading national labs. I know what it takes to actually implement technology in organizations that have to keep running while change is happening.

  • 12+ years K-12 leadership in Michigan: superintendent, COO, principal
  • AI trainer for leading national AI labs via Mercor Intelligence
  • Workshop presenter at MASA state conference on AI in education

For schools, I understand the added layer. Protecting student data is not a roadblock. It is the right instinct, and it shapes how I approach every engagement in that environment.
